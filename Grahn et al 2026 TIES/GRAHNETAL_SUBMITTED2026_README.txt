DATA-SPECIFIC INFORMATION FOR: GRAHNETAL_SUBMITTED2026.csv

- Number of variables: 16

- Number of cases/rows: 4096

- Variable List:

id: participant's id

run: trial number, values: 3-6 (1 and 2 were practice trials)

occ_nr: number of occlusion in a sequence

occ_time: occlusion time, seconds

timeInside: time spend inside the lane during occlusion, seconds

timeOutside: time spend outside the lane during occlusion, seconds

tlc: time-to-line-crossing from the start of occlusion, seconds

motion: motion platform, values: 1=on / 0=off

group: participant's group, values: 1=high-sanction group / 2=low-sanction group

rps.avg: participant's mean Risk Propensity Scale score

TLC.variability: time-to-line-crossing variability, values: 1=low variability / 2=high variability

gender: participant's gender, values: 1=male / 2=female

age: participant's age, years

prev_occ_time: previous occlusion's occlusion time, seconds

prev_timeOutside: time driven outside the lane during previous occlusion, seconds

lane.sanction: magnitude of the lane sanction, values: 0=low / 1=high


- Missing data codes: space

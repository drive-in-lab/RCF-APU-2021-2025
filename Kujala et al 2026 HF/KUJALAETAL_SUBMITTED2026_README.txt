- Number of variables: 26
- Number of cases/rows: 36790
- Variable List: 

Participant - participant id

Gender - participant gender, values: M - Male / F - Female

Age - participant age

DrivingExperienceinyears - driving experience of the participants (in years)

Drivingperyearinkms - driving by participants per year (in kilometers)

Lifetimedrivinginkms - lifetime driving by participants (in kilometers)

NbackTest - Whether participants performed any N-back test, values: None - no test / 0-back - participants did 0-back test / 2-back - participants did 2-back test

@2backTestScore - scores on 0-back or 2-back tests

RPSScore - score of risk propensity scale

Road - roads on which participants drove, values: R2 / R4

Mode - driving mode, values: manual / automated

Condition - driving condition, values: manual / automated / Automated_0-back (automated driving with 0-back test) / Automated_2-back (automated driving with 2-back test)

OcclusionSituation - driving scenarios during which participants occluded themselves, values: Trigger_Ahighway / Trigger_Child / Trigger_Ambulance / Trigger_Obstacle / Trigger_Zebra / Trigger_Nonpriority / Trigger_Parked / Trigger_Bus / Trigger_Moose / Trigger_Roundabout / Baseline 

OcclusionSequence: sequence in which the driving scenarios were presented to the participant

OcclusionDuration: time for which participants occluded themselves (in seconds)

OcclusionDistance: occluded driving distance (in kilometers)

OD_m: occluded driving distance in meters

Baseline_driving: Participants drove under baseline conditions or not, values: 1 = yes / 0 = no
